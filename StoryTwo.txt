A typewriter clacked in the dim room, each keystroke a release of words he couldn't say aloud, a solitary symphony for a world that never bothered to listen.
As he wrote he remembers times long gone, regrets never to be undone, words he wished to say.
The image of her haunts his mind. Only an image - not even a name. 
Her figure in his mind was faded from being summoned from the deepest recesses of his memories.
Her voice echoed through him "Pokemon Go to the polls", Truly could any woman be as kind as her?
