She walked into the room with a confidence that could shake skyscrapers, eyes like firewater, and a laugh that dared you to step into her chaos. 
"Kneel before me peasants!" she said.
The peasants did in fact kneel before her.
Fuled[sic] by self-righteous certainty, she took her assigned seat in the 4th grade classroom.
"Thank you Clair..." the teacher said, exasperated.
